# 1. Purpose

This document defines the business requirements for the T.N. Memorial School Digital Platform. It identifies business needs, operational goals, stakeholders, and expected outcomes before the technical design phase begins.

---

# 2. Business Vision

To establish a modern, secure, and efficient digital ecosystem that streamlines academic and administrative operations while improving communication among students, parents, teachers, and school management.

---

# 3. Business Goals

- Digitize school operations.
- Reduce paperwork.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Enhance transparency.
- Improve communication.
- Support data-driven decision making.
- Provide centralized information management.
- Enable future scalability.

---

# 4. Business Objectives

## Academic

- Manage student records.
- Manage attendance.
- Conduct examinations.
- Publish results.
- Track academic performance.

---

## Administration

- Admission Management
- Staff Management
- Timetable Management
- Certificate Management
- Document Management

---

## Financial

- Fee Collection
- Receipt Generation
- Due Management
- Financial Reporting

---

## Communication

- Notices
- Circulars
- Announcements
- Parent Communication

# 6. Business Needs

- Centralized information
- Faster report generation
- Secure user access
- Accurate records
- Real-time information
- Automated workflows
- Better communication

---

# 7. Business Constraints

- Limited implementation budget
- Internet dependency
- User training required
- Existing manual records
- Data migration effort

---

# 8. Business Benefits

| Area | Expected Benefit |
|------|------------------|
| Administration | Faster operations |
| Academics | Better record management |
| Finance | Accurate fee tracking |
| Communication | Instant notifications |
| Reporting | Real-time reports |

# 9. Business Success Criteria

- Reduced paperwork
- Improved efficiency
- Increased data accuracy
- Better reporting
- Faster communication
- Higher user satisfaction

# 10. Assumptions
